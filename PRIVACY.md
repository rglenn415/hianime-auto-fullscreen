# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** December 2024

## What Data We Collect

**None.** This extension collects no personal information, browsing history, or usage data.

## What We Store Locally

The extension stores your preference settings on your device only:
- Manual episode fullscreen toggle (on/off)
- Autoplay episode fullscreen toggle (on/off)

This data never leaves your browser and is only accessible to this extension.

## Permissions

**activeTab** - Required to detect video players on HiAnime pages you visit
**storage** - Required to save your fullscreen preferences locally

## Third Parties

This extension does not:
- Send data to any servers
- Use analytics or tracking
- Share data with third parties
- Connect to external services

## Open Source

The extension code is publicly available on GitHub for review and verification.

## Contact

Report issues at: https://github.com/rglenn415/hianime-auto-fullscreen/issues

## Changes

If this policy changes, the version number will be updated on the Firefox Add-ons store listing.

## Full Description (5000 characters max)

Enhance your HiAnime binge-watching experience with automatic fullscreen! This extension intelligently detects episode changes and triggers fullscreen mode automatically, with separate controls for manual episode selection and autoplay transitions.

### ‚ú® Key Features

**Two Independent Toggles:**
- **Manual Episodes** - Auto-fullscreen when you click a new episode
- **Autoplay Episodes** - Stay fullscreen during binge-watching when autoplay triggers

**Smart Detection:**
- Monitors video source changes
- Detects autoplay transitions
- Distinguishes between manual selection and autoplay
- Maintains fullscreen across episode changes

**Multi-Monitor Support:**
- Works seamlessly with multiple displays
- Respects your monitor placement
- Maintains fullscreen on the correct screen

**Privacy First:**
- No data collection
- No analytics or tracking
- Settings stored locally only
- Completely offline operation

### üéÆ How to Use

1. **Install the extension**
2. **Visit HiAnime** and play any episode
3. **Click the extension icon** to access settings
4. **Choose your preferences:**
   - Turn ON both toggles for full auto (default)
   - Manual only: Fullscreen on click, exit on autoplay
   - Autoplay only: Start windowed, stay fullscreen during binge
   - Both OFF: Complete manual control

### üìã Common Configurations

**Full Auto (Default):**
Both toggles ON - Always fullscreen, perfect for hands-free binge-watching

**Manual Control:**
Manual ON, Autoplay OFF - Fullscreen when clicking episodes, gives you a break between autoplay transitions

**Binge Mode:**
Manual OFF, Autoplay ON - Start windowed, but stay fullscreen once you're settled in

**Fully Disabled:**
Both OFF - You control fullscreen manually

### üîß Technical Details

- Detects video player automatically
- Works with HiAnime's player library
- Monitors multiple signals for reliable detection
- Console logging available for debugging (disabled by default)

# Privacy Policy for HiAnime Auto Fullscreen

**Last Updated:** December 30, 2025

## Overview

HiAnime Auto Fullscreen ("the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains what data the extension collects, how it's used, and your rights.

## Data Collection

### What We Collect

The extension stores **only** the following data locally on your device:

1. **User Preferences** (stored in browser local storage):
   - `enableManualEpisode` (boolean) - Whether to auto-fullscreen manually selected episodes
   - `enableAutoplayEpisode` (boolean) - Whether to auto-fullscreen during autoplay

### What We DO NOT Collect

The extension does **NOT** collect, store, or transmit:

- ❌ Personal information (name, email, etc.)
- ❌ Browsing history
- ❌ Video viewing habits
- ❌ Cookies or tracking data
- ❌ Analytics or telemetrics
- ❌ Any data sent to external servers

## How Data is Used

Your preferences are used **only** to:

- Remember your fullscreen settings between browser sessions
- Control when auto-fullscreen should trigger

All data stays on **your device** and is never transmitted anywhere.

## Data Storage

- **Where:** All data is stored locally in your Firefox browser using the `browser.storage.local` API
- **Access:** Only this extension can access its own storage data
- **Duration:** Data persists until you uninstall the extension or clear browser data

## Permissions Explanation

The extension requests the following permissions:

### `storage`
**Why:** To save your preferences (manual/autoplay fullscreen settings)
**Data:** Only the two boolean settings mentioned above

### `activeTab`
**Why:** To access HiAnime pages and detect video players
**Data:** No data is collected; only used to interact with video elements on HiAnime

## Third-Party Access

- **No third parties** have access to any data
- **No external servers** are contacted
- **No analytics services** are used
- The extension is **completely offline** (except for loading HiAnime itself)

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **View** your settings: Click the extension icon
- **Modify** your settings: Toggle the switches in the popup
- **Delete** your data: Uninstall the extension or clear browser storage

## Changes to Stored Data

The only way your settings change is when **you** toggle them in the extension popup.
